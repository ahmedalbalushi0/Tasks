Elara, a spirited young villager with wild chestnut curls and eyes like storm-lit skies, was known for her boundless curiosity and fearless heart. When she uncovered the mysterious map hidden beneath her grandmother’s floorboards, a thrill surged through her—this was no ordinary parchment, and her adventure was only beginning.

Shadow, a sleek, silver-furred wolf with eyes like amber fire, had been by Elara’s side since she rescued him as a pup. Silent and watchful, he moved like mist through the forest, sensing danger before it struck. Fiercely loyal, Shadow was more than a companion—he was her guardian and guide.
